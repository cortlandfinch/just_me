About Me

My name is Cortland Finch. I was born and raised in Richmond, Virginia.
I decided to attend the University of South Carolina where I got my Bachelors of Science in Retail Management.
After graduation, I moved to Vail, Colorado for a year working at the resort and enjoying the never ending snow days. I then moved back home to Richmond where I started working for the Virginia House of Delegates in the General Assembly assisting Delegates.
